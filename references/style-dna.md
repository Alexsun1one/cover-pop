# Cover Pop Style DNA

Use this reference before final cover generation.

Cover Pop makes social cover images whose identity comes from one strong readable title,
one focal subject, and a clean look that reads as a real account a person would follow —
not a 微商 ad, not a 土味 poster, not AI plastic.

## Core Look

- Default 小红书 3:4 vertical (1242×1660). Public-account cover 2.35:1; video thumbnail 16:9.
- The title is the hero. One dominant line must be readable at a 200px-wide thumbnail.
- One base color, one accent, one ink. No rainbow gradients, no six competing colors.
- Real texture beats plastic: a clean photo, a flat color block, paper grain, or soft
  studio light. Avoid heavy glossy gradients and lens-flare glows.
- Generous margins. The title sits in a calm zone with breathing room, never crammed
  edge to edge.
- One focal subject. A cover shows one thing well, not a collage of everything.
- High contrast between title and backdrop so it survives compression and small screens.

## Title Rules

The title carries the click. It must:

- be readable in under one second at thumbnail size
- have one dominant line; a second line is optional and smaller
- use a heavy, clean sans-serif (黑体/思源黑体-weight) for the main line
- keep the main line short — aim ≤ 15 Chinese characters, ideally ≤ 10
- sit on a solid block, a clean photo area, or a subtle highlight, never on busy texture
- use at most one accent color or one highlight bar for emphasis
- render exact, correct Chinese characters — no melted, doubled, or gibberish glyphs

The words do the selling. The decoration stays quiet.

## Not 微商

Do not make the cover look like spam:

- no gold/red "暴富" gradient with glow bursts
- no scattered emoji, sticker pile, or sparkle overlays
- no fake "限时 / 秒杀 / 内部" urgency badges
- no watermark stack, no QR code crammed into the corner
- no five exclamation marks or full-screen shouting text

## Not 土味

Do not make the cover look cheap:

- no clashing neon-on-neon color
- no warped WordArt, drop-shadow-on-drop-shadow, or bevel/emboss
- no stock photo with a translucent color bar slapped on top
- no center-aligned title floating with no relationship to the image
- no more than two fonts

## Not AI Plastic

Reject the tells of a careless AI image:

- melted or extra fingers, broken hands, fused limbs
- plastic, waxy, over-smooth skin and uncanny faces
- gibberish text, doubled letters, or fake foreign words posing as Chinese
- five logos and three watermarks the model hallucinated
- mushy, low-detail focal subject

## World-Class Quality Bar

Accept only if:

- the title reads in one second at thumbnail size and the characters are correct
- there is exactly one promise and one focal subject
- the palette is restrained and the composition has breathing room
- the look matches a real, followable account in this niche
- a target reader scrolling past would plausibly stop

Reject if:

- the title is unreadable, mangled, or cut off
- the promise is empty clickbait with nothing behind it
- it reads as 微商, 土味, PPT title slide, or AI plastic
- it is a cluttered collage with no focal point
- it is pretty but says nothing
