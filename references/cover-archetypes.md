# Cover Archetypes

Choose one archetype per cover. The topic picks the archetype, not the other way around.
Each archetype has a job, a layout, and a title pattern.

## 大字封面 / Big-Type

Use for: an opinion, a declaration, a single strong line, a hot take, a counter-intuitive claim.

Layout: the title fills 50–70% of the frame on a solid or softly textured block. Tiny or
no imagery. One accent color or one highlight bar on the key word.

Title pattern: a complete, confident sentence. The image IS the words.

Example: 「不是你不努力，是方法错了」 · 「30 岁才懂的 5 件事」 · 「这才是 AI 的正确用法」

## 反差 / 避坑封面 / Contrast

Use for: a mistake to avoid, a wrong-vs-right, a before/after, a fix, a transformation.

Layout: split frame (left/right or top/bottom). One side ✗ wrong/before, one side ✓
right/after. A clear divider. Red tab on the wrong side, green/blue on the right side.

Title pattern: "别再…了" / "从 X 到 Y" / "踩了 N 个坑后". Name the wrong thing the reader
is doing.

Example: 「别再这样发朋友圈了」 · 「装修踩坑 vs 避坑」 · 「改了一个词，阅读量翻倍」

## 清单 / 数字封面 / List

Use for: a roundup, a resource pack, "N 个…", a checklist, a toolkit.

Layout: a big number is the anchor (the "5" / "10" is large and colored). Title beside or
under the number. Optional small tidy icons or thumbnails hinting at the items.

Title pattern: "N 个 + 收益". The number sets a finite, scannable promise.

Example: 「7 个被低估的 AI 工具」 · 「3 步搞定 PPT 排版」 · 「10 个免费图库」

## 教程 / 步骤封面 / How-To

Use for: a tutorial, a recipe, a how-to, a "手把手".

Layout: show the finished result as the focal subject (the plated dish, the final
design), with a "教程 / 手把手 / 保姆级" tag. The result sells the method.

Title pattern: "手把手教你 X" / "X 保姆级教程" / "新手也能 X". Promise the outcome and ease.

Example: 「10 分钟搞定的快手菜」 · 「保姆级 Notion 模板教程」 · 「新手第一次也能剪好」

## 情绪 / 共鸣封面 / Emotional

Use for: a personal essay, a mood, a relatable moment, a feeling the reader is in now.

Layout: one quiet evocative image (a window, a desk, a street at dusk) + a soft, honest
title. Lower contrast, calmer palette than a 大字 cover. The feeling leads.

Title pattern: a line the reader would think to themselves. First or second person, no
selling tone.

Example: 「成年人的崩溃，都是静音的」 · 「今天也想辞职的第 200 天」 · 「我把房间收拾干净后」

## 实拍 / 好物封面 / Product Shot

Use for: a product, food, place, outfit, or haul.

Layout: a clean, well-lit real photo of the object as the focal subject. Title small and
in a corner or along an edge — the object is the hook, not the words.

Title pattern: a short tag — the object name + one benefit, or just a vibe word.

Example: 「这个 9.9 的好物真香」 · 「打工人的桌面好物」 · 「平价但高级的穿搭」

Use the user's real photo. If the user owns the object, composite their real photo onto a
clean ground or relight it — do not let the model invent the product, especially branded
packaging or any text on the item (wrong logos and mangled brand text are the classic AI tell
and misrepresent the product). Generate from scratch only for generic, unbranded objects.

## 盘点 / 合集封面 / Grid

Use for: a collection, a comparison, a "盘点", a versus.

Layout: a tidy grid (2×2, 3×3) of clean thumbnails with a small title bar across the top
or bottom. Even spacing, consistent crops.

Title pattern: "盘点 / 合集 / 谁更值" + the category.

Example: 「2024 必看的 6 部纪录片」 · 「5 款平价粉底液横评」 · 「年度好用 App 盘点」

## Choosing Notes

- Declaration or opinion → 大字.
- Mistake, fix, or transformation → 反差.
- Finite list or toolkit → 清单/数字.
- Step-by-step result → 教程.
- Mood or essay → 情绪.
- One desirable object → 实拍.
- Many items compared → 盘点.

When two fit, pick the one whose hook is more concrete for this exact reader. Never stack
three archetypes onto one cover — that is how covers become cluttered collages.
