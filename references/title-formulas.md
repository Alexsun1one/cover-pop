# Title Formulas

The title earns the tap. On a cover, the words matter more than the art. Write the title
first, then design around it.

## What a good cover title does

- names a specific reader or situation, so the right person feels "这说的是我"
- promises one concrete payoff, not a vague vibe
- opens a curiosity gap the reader wants to close
- reads in under one second, ≤ 15 Chinese characters on the main line

## Formula bank

Use one. Do not stack three.

### 1. 数字 + 收益

`{N} 个 / {N} 步 + {具体收益}` — finite and scannable.

「7 个让效率翻倍的快捷键」 · 「3 步写出不尬的朋友圈文案」

### 2. 点名人群

`{人群} + {他们的痛点/收益}` — the reader self-selects.

「打工人必备的 5 个摸鱼神器」 · 「i 人也能用的社交话术」

### 3. 反常识 / 反差

`不是 X，而是 Y` / `别再 X 了` — break an assumption.

「不是你不自律，是目标定错了」 · 「别再用模板做 PPT 了」

### 4. 结果前置

`{惊人结果} + {方法暗示}` — lead with the outcome.

「改了一句话，简历通过率翻倍」 · 「这样拍，手机也能出大片」

### 5. 提问 / 缺口

`{读者想知道答案的问题}` — open a loop.

「为什么你的笔记没人看？」 · 「月薪 3 千和 3 万的差距在哪？」

### 6. 保姆级 / 零门槛

`{零基础人群} + 也能 + {结果}` — lower the barrier.

「完全不会画画也能做出的封面」 · 「第一次做也不会翻车的蛋糕」

### 7. 清单承诺 / 一篇讲清

`{话题} + 一篇讲清 / 全在这了` — promise completeness.

「租房避坑，这一篇全讲清」 · 「新手买相机，看这篇就够」

## Title craft rules

- Cut every word that does not change the meaning. 12 strong characters beat 20 soft ones.
- Use one concrete noun the reader can picture, not an abstraction.
- Front-load the hook word; the strongest word should not be last.
- One claim per title. If you have two, make one the small sub-line.
- A sub-line (≤ 10 字) can add specificity or audience: under 「3 步搞定排版」 → 「PPT 小白专用」.
- **Payoff map (write it, do not skip).** For every title, write the concrete thing the note
  delivers that makes the title true: title 「7 个被低估的 AI 工具」 → payoff map must list at least
  the count and kind ("7 named tools, each with a use case"). If you cannot fill the map from the
  actual topic/draft — if the number, the named tools, or the result do not exist behind the tap —
  the title is 标题党空心: cut the claim down to what the content actually delivers. A title whose
  payoff map is empty or smaller than the title promises is rejected, not softened.

## Anti-patterns

- 标题党空心: a shocking line with nothing behind it. The reader taps once, then never
  trusts the account. Always make the promise real.
- 太抽象: 「关于成长的一些思考」 — picture nothing, promise nothing.
- 太长: more than ~15 characters on the main line dies at thumbnail size.
- 全是形容词: 「超级无敌好用」 says less than one concrete noun.
- 标点轰炸: 「！！！」 reads as spam, not emphasis.
